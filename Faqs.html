FAQs

Our admission requirements include a completed application form, academic records, and an interview. For detailed information, please visit our admissions page.

Parents can track their child’s academic progress through our online portal, where grades and reports are regularly updated.

We have a zero-tolerance policy towards bullying. Incidents can be reported to any staff member, and they will be addressed promptly and effectively.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to participate in school activities through our Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) and volunteer programs. Check our events calendar for upcoming opportunities.

We offer various resources and support services for students with special needs, including individualized education plans (IEPs) and access to specialized staff. For more details, please contact our special education department.
